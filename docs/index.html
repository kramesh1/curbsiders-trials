Data extracted from The Curbsiders Podcast show notes. For educational use only.

Teaching pearls are pulled verbatim from show notes, but trial summaries and evidence links are AI-extracted and may contain errors. Model-suggested evidence links are only shown once a reviewer has explicitly signed off on that pearl (unsigned-off suggestions are withheld); everything else here is unreviewed model or automated output. This is a study aid, not a substitute for reading primary sources or an authoritative clinical reference, and it is not medical advice -- do not use it to make patient-care decisions.
